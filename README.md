# Filters

**14 artistic filters. Fully adjustable intensity. Non-destructive.**

---

## What it does

Filters gives you a curated collection of colour and tone presets — from timeless black-and-white to cinematic film looks. Apply any filter at any strength, then continue editing. Nothing is baked in; you can change the intensity or remove the filter entirely at any point.

---

## Features

- 14 filters across five categories: B&W, Blur, Tone, Color, Film, and Exposure
- Adjustable intensity (0–100 %) per filter
- Fully non-destructive — stored as a single history entry
- Instant live preview as you drag the intensity slider

---

## Available filters

| Category | Filters |
|---|---|
| B&W | Black & White, Desaturate |
| Blur | Soften |
| Tone | Hard Light, Soft Light |
| Color | Vivid, Warm, Cool |
| Film | Old Photo, Matte, Faded, Cross Process |
| Exposure | Lighten, Darken |

---

## How to use

1. Open a photo in PhotoDewey
2. Open the **Filters** panel in the sidebar
3. Click a filter to apply it at full intensity
4. Drag the **Intensity** slider to taste
5. The filter is added to your history — undo or swap it at any time
